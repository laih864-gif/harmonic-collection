A website exploring the warmth and texture of physical objects in a digital world.

In a time when so much of life happens on screens, we forget how meaningful it can be to touch, hold, and notice the objects around us. The Warmth of the Tangible is a collection of small studies about physical things — records, film, letters, books, handmade objects — and the memories, textures, and moments they carry. Each entry looks at a different way we interact with the material world, asking how slowness, imperfection, and touch can make us feel more present.
